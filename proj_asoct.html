Angle-Closure Glaucoma Screening in Anterior Segment OCT

Introduction:





Angle-closure glaucoma is a major cause of irreversible visual impairment and can be identiﬁed by measuring the anterior chamber angle (ACA) of the eye. The ACA can be viewed clearly through anterior segment optical coherence tomography (AS-OCT). To address this problem, we propose a whole system for automatic AS-OCT segmentation, measurement, and screening.
