Welcome to Runmin Cong's Homepage

Hi！My name is Runmin Cong (丛润民), a third year Ph.D candidate at School of Electrical and Information Engineering, Tianjin University, P.R. China, supervised by Profs. Qingming Huang and Jianjun Lei. From Dec. 2016 to Feb. 2017, I worked with Prof. Weisi Lin (IEEE/IET Fellow) as a visiting student in Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore.
